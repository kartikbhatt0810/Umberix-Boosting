FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Is my account safe during the boost? +
Yes. Our boosters use VPN matching to your region and play conservatively. We've completed 500+ boosts with zero bans. You'll be notified before and after we log in.
Is the boost 100% guaranteed? +
No boost service can promise 100% results — Minecraft PvP is live and competitive. We hold a 95% success rate. Results depend on server conditions and competition level.
How long does a boost take? +
Most boosts are done within 24 hours. HT3 orders are on priority and often delivered faster. You'll get live updates throughout your ticket so you always know where things stand.
What's the difference between LT3 and HT3? +
LT3 (Low Tier 3) puts you in the lower range of Tier 3 — a solid starting point. HT3 (High Tier 3) pushes you into the upper bracket, competing against the strongest Tier 3 players.
How do I pay with crypto or GPay? +
Open a ticket in our Discord and tell us which method you prefer. We'll share our BTC or LTC wallet address, or a UPI ID for GPay (INR). Your boost starts immediately after payment confirms.
